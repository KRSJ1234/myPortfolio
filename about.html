ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Kevin Jose!

Originally from Kerala, India, I've pursued a dynamic career in technology and innovation, culminating in my current role as a Software Engineer at Cisco Systems. My educational background includes a Bachelor's in Electronics and Electrical Engineering with a minor in Product Design from IIT Guwahati, a foundation that has been instrumental in my professional growth, encompassing computer networking, backend distributed systems engineering, digital electrical system design, artificial intelligence, and UX/UI development.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I am a passionate basketball player, having competed in state and national tournaments, and an avid reader with interests ranging from history to non-fiction. My formative years in the National Cadet Corps instilled in me a strong sense of discipline and values. My creativity and love for design in various forms have now led me to transition into product design, marking a new and exciting phase in my career since 2023.
