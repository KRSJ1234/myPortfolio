ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Kevin Jose!

Originally from Kerala, India, I've pursued a dynamic career in technology and innovation, culminating in my current role as a Software Engineer at Cisco Systems. My educational background includes a Bachelor's in Electronics and Electrical Engineering with a minor in Product Design from IIT Guwahati. Beyond my technical pursuits, I am a passionate basketball player, having competed in various state and national tournaments. I currently play as a point guard for the Cisco Corporate basketball team and St. Raphael's Basketball Club in my hometown. As an avid reader, my interests span history, technology, sports, and other areas of non-fiction. My creativity and love for design in various forms have now led me to transition into product design, marking a new and exciting phase in my career since 2023.
