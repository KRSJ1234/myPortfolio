The Wall of Smiles
Welcome to my favorite section of this portfolio. This collection is very close to my heart as it features genuine smiles of those who mean the world to me, captured in their happiest moments. On days when I need a boost, a stroll through this gallery instantly uplifts my spirits.
You might wonder about the choice of grayscale for these photos. It began unexpectedly one evening when my phone, set to enter a grayscale mode post 11pm, transformed these images into shades of gray. To my surprise, the smiles in these photos seemed more profound, each frame echoing a cherished memory with greater intensity. I realized that without the distraction of colors, the true essence of these moments shone brighter. This revelation inspired me to present all these portraits in grayscale, accentuating the smiles and the stories they tell.
